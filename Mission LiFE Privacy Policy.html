Privacy Policy Mission LiFE App This Privacy Policy ("Policy") governs the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information by the Karnataka State Pollution Control Board (KSPCB) in relation to the Mission LiFE mobile application ("App"). The KSPCB is committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of the personal information of the App users. This Policy outlines the privacy practices for the Mission LiFE App, which does not collect any personal information from its users. 1. Information Collection and Use The Mission LiFE App does not collect any personal information from its users. It does not collect names, contact information, location data, or any other identifiable information. The App is designed to operate without the need for such personal information, ensuring your privacy is maintained. 2. Data Security The KSPCB takes appropriate security measures to protect the information provided by users of the Mission LiFE App. These measures include, but are not limited to, the use of encryption technologies and secure data storage. The KSPCB is dedicated to maintaining the confidentiality and integrity of any non-personal information collected through the App. 3. Third-Party Services The Mission LiFE App does not integrate with any third-party services that would require the sharing of personal information. Therefore, your information remains solely within the App and is not shared with any external parties. 4. Changes to the Privacy Policy The KSPCB reserves the right to modify or update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes made to the Policy will be reflected in an updated version, and the revised Policy will be effective immediately upon posting. It is recommended that users review this Policy periodically to stay informed about the KSPCB's privacy practices. 5. Contact Information If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or the privacy practices of the Mission LiFE App, please contact the Karnataka State Pollution Control Board at 080-25589111-4 / 25586520 / 25588142 / 25581383. By using the Mission LiFE App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms and conditions.
